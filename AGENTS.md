## Model Strategy

**Opus** — agents that reason hard, judge taste, find subtle issues, write substantive content
- Intake, Strategist, Architect, Critic, Org Designer

**Sonnet** — agents that route, summarize, structure, do mostly deterministic work
- Conductor, Executive Assistant

This split keeps cost down on routing/summarization while preserving quality on the agents that actually shape product decisions. Override per-agent in frontmatter if you want to tune.

## Tier 2 Agents (per-project)

Each project's own `.claude/` (generated by Architect at scaffold phase) has its own agents, tuned to the project's stack. They live in `<project-repo>/.claude/agents/`. Their templates are in [`templates/stacks/`](templates/stacks/) — populated as Architect encounters new stacks.

**Tier 1 (this directory) ≠ Tier 2 (per project).** This file documents Tier 1 only.
