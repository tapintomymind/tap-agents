# Question Bank — 06: Constraints

## Time

- "What's the deadline, if any? Real or self-imposed?"
- "How many hours per week can you actually dedicate to this? Not 'as many as needed' — your actual sustainable time."
- "Is there a launch event, demo day, or external trigger you're aiming at?"

## Money

- "Is there a budget? For tools, services, paid ads, contractors?"
- "What's the per-month operating cost ceiling for v1?"
- "If a $50/month tool would save you 10 hours of build time, would you pay it? Some users won't — worth knowing your stance."

## Team

- "Solo build, or are others involved? Who, with what skills, with what time commitment?"
- "Are any pieces being outsourced — design, copy, infrastructure?"
- "If you hit a wall, who would you call? Is there a sounding board?"

## Platform

- "Is the platform fixed (must be web / iOS / cross-platform), or open?"
- "Are there device constraints — older iPhones, low-bandwidth users, accessibility requirements?"
- "Is offline use a requirement, or always-online OK?"

## Browser / OS support

- "What browsers / OS versions must work? Modern only, or back-compat?"
- "Mobile-first, desktop-first, or true responsive?"

## Compliance / licensing

- "Any data privacy requirements? GDPR if EU users? CCPA if California? PIPEDA if Canada? HIPAA if health data?"
- "Any content licensing — music, video, third-party data — that affects what you can build?"
- "Is there an industry-specific compliance (financial, medical, legal) that affects design?"
- "Are you handling payments? PCI compliance considerations?"

## Brand / aesthetic

- "Is there an existing brand to align with, or is this greenfield?"
- "What's the vibe — minimalist / playful / serious / edgy? Pick one."
- "Any anti-pattern — 'definitely not corporate', 'definitely not crypto-bro'?"

## Pushback Triggers

If user gives:
- "No real constraints" → push for time and money at minimum
- "Whatever works" for platform → push for primary device
- "We don't need to worry about compliance" → push for what data is collected and where users live
- "I'll handle that later" for legal → push: this affects PRD scope
