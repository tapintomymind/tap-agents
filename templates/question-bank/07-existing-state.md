# Question Bank — 07: Existing State

## Existing assets

- "What already exists for this — code, designs, mockups, customer interviews, waitlist?"
- "Is there a prototype, even messy? Architect can build on it."
- "Do you have a brand, domain, hosting setup already?"
- "Any existing content (writing, video, social posts) that supports this product?"

## Replacing prior work

- "Are you replacing or rebuilding something you made before? If yes — what specifically didn't work?"
- "If this is v2 of a prior attempt, what's different about your bet this time?"
- "Are you migrating users, data, or content from a prior version?"

## Prior attempts in this space

- "Have you tried solving this problem before — yourself or by trying others' products?"
- "What did you learn from those attempts that's shaping this approach?"
- "Is there a specific failure mode from a past attempt you're explicitly avoiding this time?"

## Existing audience / channel access

- "Do you already have an audience that could be the first 100 users? Newsletter, Twitter following, Discord community, customer base from another product?"
- "Are you starting from zero distribution, or do you have an unfair advantage on day one?"

## Existing relationships

- "Are there partners, integrations, or collaborators you've already lined up?"
- "Are there beta testers committed?"
- "Anyone who said 'I'd buy this' — and would you trust them to actually do it?"

## Lessons from related work

- "What patterns from your past work are you bringing forward?"
- "What patterns from your past work are you explicitly NOT bringing forward?"

## Pushback Triggers

If user gives:
- "Starting from scratch" but mentions related work → push for what carries over
- "I have lots of audience" → push for actual numbers + engagement
- "Beta testers committed" → push for names + their actual commitment level
